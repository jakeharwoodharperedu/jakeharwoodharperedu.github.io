Summary

While completing these activities, I learned about the structure and relationships of tables within a database,
how to identify primary keys, and how to query specific information based on various conditions.
This knowledge is important for understanding how to utilize databases effectively.
In my potential future projects, I could use these skills to navigate databases using SQL, design efficient database
schemas, and write accurate queries.
