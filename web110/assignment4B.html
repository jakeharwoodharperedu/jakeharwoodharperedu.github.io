Completing TutorialsPoint
I completed the tutorials from GCFLearnFree and TutorialsPoint that focused on internet safety and security.
The GCFLearnFree tutorial emphasized best practices for maintaining internet safety,
such as creating strong passwords, recognizing phishing attempts, and ensuring software updates.
The TutorialsPoint internet security tutorial covered various security threats, including malware, phishing,
and other cyber attacks, and talked about measures to protect personal information online.
These tutorials provided a thorough understanding of the types of threats present on the internet.

Testing Firewall Effectiveness
The second activity I did focused on testing firewall effectiveness using Gibson Research's ShieldsUP!.
This involved scanning my system for open ports and vulnerabilities to assess the firewall's.
ShieldsUP scanned for potential security vulnerabilities and gathered all that it could from my IP address.
I tried it's scan while using a VPN, then again with it off.
This hands-on test allowed me to see firsthand how vulnerabilities could be identified and addressed.
